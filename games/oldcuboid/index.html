Use WASD to move the red cuboid into the BLACK hole to reach the next level. BLUE and GREEN buttons open and close DARKER TILES that may or may not help you complete the level. You have to be upright on a GREEN button to activate it. Standing upright on a ORANGE TILE will reset the level. The number in the parentheses indicate the number of completed levels

WASD - move
J - Last level
K - Reload level
L - Next level

If you use J, K or L you will lose all statistics
